02

to create a video from text use, 

/create

plus text to create a video, then 

-fps 24 -ar 16:9

for frame rate (8 - 24) and aspect ratio

03

To create a video from an image use, 

/create title of thing

You can add an arg for camera movement zoom in and out 

/create title of thing -camera zoom in

click the + 1 at the end to add the image
