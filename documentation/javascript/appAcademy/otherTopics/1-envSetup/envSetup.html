Enviroment Setup - Mac

 Make sure your mac is up-to-date

  • Click the Apple Menu in the upper left
  • Select "About this Mac"
  • Click the "Software Update" Button
  • Follow the instructions in the "Software Upgrade" window and complete all available updates and/or upgrades.

 Follow the setup instructions contained in the App Academy Unified Setup Instructions
